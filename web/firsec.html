Before Starting

Hold your Rubik’s Mini such that WHITE tile is facing the ceiling.

  • The WHITE tile will be on the UP face.
  • The RED tile will be on the FRONT face.
  • The BLUE tile will be on the RIGHT face.

STEP 1

Find the corner piece that has a WHITE tile, a BLUE tile and an ORANGE tile.


STEP 2

Hold your Rubik’s Mini to match one of the pictures below (with the WHITE-BLUE-ORANGE corner on the right) and follow the algorithm:


R'D'R

STEP 3

Twist the DOWN face until your Rubik’s Mini matches one of the pictures below ie  D.


Holding the FRONT facing you, follow this algorithm:

R

Holding the FRONT facing you, follow this algorithm:

R'D'R

Holding the FRONT facing you, follow this algorithm:
