Before Starting

HOLD YOUR RUBIK’S MINI Begin such that YELLOW on the UP face.


STEP 1

Twist the top layer until the colored tiles of two corners match the bottom layer,As shown in next cases(ie U).

STEP 2

Hold your Rubik’s Mini so it matches one of the cases.

CASE 1

ADJACENT CORNERS (NEXT TO EACH OTHER)


CASE 2

DIAGONAL CORNERS (NOT TOUCHING EACH OTHER)


STEP 3

Follow this algorithm for Case 1 and Case 2

R'FR'B2RF'R'B2R2U'

STEP 4

If your Rubik’s Mini is not solved, you need to repeat STEP 1-3.
